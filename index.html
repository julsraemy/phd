Through and beyond an Actor-Network Theory (ANT) lens

This website is under construction.

It will be about my doctoral thesis in Digital Humanities titled Linked Open Usable Data standards for Citizen Science: perspectives on Interoperability and Knowledge Representation through and beyond an Actor-Network Theory lens (working tile) which is undertaken at the University of Basel in the context of the Participatory Knowledge Practices in Analogue and Digital Image Archives (PIA) research project.

The idea is to keep four digital notebooks (here named "log, cards, Ad libitum and effects") as hinted by Bruno Latour in his 2005 book Reassembling the Social ("Back to basics: a list of notebooks" - pp. 133-135).
